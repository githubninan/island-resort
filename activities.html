Activities

Idyllic beaches and islands, swathes of lowland jungle and simple, uncomplicated tourist destinations characterise this province. Still, there are several options for those who like to get out and about in this beautiful province. From rock climbing on the tall limestone cliffs typical to this area to snorkelling in the crystal waters around stunningly beautiful small islands to trekking through the lush vegetation of the rainforest, you surely will find something to do.
